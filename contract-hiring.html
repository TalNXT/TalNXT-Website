Flexible Models

C2C & C2H Solutions

Choose the contract hiring model that best fits your needs—Corporate-to-Corporate (C2C) for business entities or Contract-to-Hire (C2H) with the option to convert to full-time employees.

Corporate-to-Corporate (C2C)

Ideal for businesses looking to engage talent through corporate entities. We facilitate the entire C2C engagement process, ensuring proper contracts, invoicing, and compliance.

  • Business-to-business engagement model
  • Simplified invoicing and payment process
  • Tax and compliance handled by contractor entity
  • Perfect for project-based or long-term engagements

Contract-to-Hire (C2H)

The best of both worlds—start with a contract engagement and convert to full-time employment once you're confident about the fit. Test the waters before making a permanent commitment.

  • Trial period before permanent commitment
  • Assess technical skills and cultural fit over time
  • Seamless conversion process when ready
  • Reduced hiring risk and better decision-making
Conversion Support

Seamless Conversion from Contract to Full-Time

Once you've worked with a contract employee and decided they're a great fit, we provide complete support in converting them to a full-time employee (FTE). Our conversion process is designed to be smooth, efficient, and hassle-free.

Assisted Conversion Process

We handle all paperwork, documentation, salary negotiations, and administrative requirements for a seamless transition from contract to FTE.

Zero Disruption to Work

The conversion happens smoothly without interrupting ongoing work. The employee continues in the same role with the same team.

Complete HR Handover

We provide a complete handover of all employee records, performance data, and documentation to your HR team for smooth integration.

Why C2H Works

Get to Know Employees and Culture Fit Over Time

One of the biggest advantages of Contract-to-Hire is the ability to assess both technical capabilities and cultural fit over several months before making a permanent commitment. This reduces hiring risks and ensures better long-term matches.

Real-World Performance Assessment

During the contract period, you observe employees in real work situations—how they handle challenges, collaborate with teams, manage deadlines, and contribute to projects. This practical assessment goes beyond interview impressions.

  • Observe work quality and deliverables over time
  • Evaluate problem-solving and adaptability skills
  • Assess reliability, accountability, and work ethic
Cultural Fit Evaluation

Over the contract period, you get to see how employees fit with your company culture, values, and team dynamics. This extended evaluation period helps ensure alignment before committing to a permanent role.

  • Assess alignment with company values and mission
  • Evaluate communication style and team integration
  • Observe long-term compatibility and growth potential
The Power of Time-Based Assessment

Traditional interviews can only reveal so much. By working with contract employees for several months, you gain deep insights into their capabilities, work style, and cultural fit. This extended evaluation period significantly reduces hiring mistakes and increases the success rate of permanent conversions. When you convert a contract employee to FTE, you're making a decision based on proven performance and observed fit, not just interview impressions.
