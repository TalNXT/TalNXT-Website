Complete Process

End-to-End Support Till Onboarding

We take complete ownership of your recruitment process from requisition to candidate onboarding, ensuring seamless experience for both you and candidates.

1
Requisition & Planning

We understand your requirements, create detailed job descriptions, and develop recruitment strategies tailored to each role.

2
Sourcing & Screening

AI-powered candidate sourcing across multiple channels, followed by comprehensive screening and initial assessments by our recruiters.

3
Interview Coordination

We schedule all interviews, coordinate with hiring managers and interview panels, and ensure smooth communication throughout.

4
Offer Management

We handle offer negotiations, background checks, reference verifications, and all pre-joining formalities on your behalf.

5
Onboarding Support

We coordinate with your HR team to ensure smooth onboarding, document collection, and provide candidate handover for seamless integration.
