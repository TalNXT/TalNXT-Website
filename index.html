Our Story

TalNXT was born from a simple realization: traditional recruitment misses the mark because recruiters haven't walked in your shoes. Our founders have been interview panelists, people managers, and recruitment heads at leading tech companies.

We know what makes a candidate great—not just on paper, but in practice. We understand why offers get accepted or declined. And we've built a process that combines AI efficiency with human insight to deliver better results.

Our journey started when we noticed a gap in the market: companies needed recruiters who truly understood tech culture, candidate motivations, and what makes teams successful. Today, we're proud to have helped scale over 100 tech teams with a focus on quality, speed, and genuine partnership.
